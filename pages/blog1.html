How Artificial Intelligence Companies Boomed the Price of RAM

Written by Von Andrei Toledo

Due to the rise of Artificial Intelligence (AI), the RAM market has been significantly shifted. Most of the AI companies have been buying a lot of RAM to upgrade their AI apps or websites. Therefore, RAM prices have skyrocketed in the last few years. Some other companies also started their own AI, which again raised the prices of RAM, an example is Nvidia.

As a student who solely depends on my allowance given by my parents, this hurts my pockets a lot. Without a stable income, it is hard to buy RAM nowadays. From 6,000 pesos for RAM sticks to now a whopping 9-10,000 pesos. That's just an estimation from what I saw in the online PC shops like PC Express. It's a lot harder now to buy RAM because a lot of people are also scalping them and selling them for a higher price than the SRP.

In conclusion, it's good that we have AI now, but there are also a lot of downsides for normal people. Like RAM prices skyrocketing and limited RAM productions. So I’m saying for people like me who don't have a stable income, it's hard to upgrade my setup without begging my parents for money.
